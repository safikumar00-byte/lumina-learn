# What is Psychology? A Simple Definition

:::definition
Psychology is the scientific study of the mind and behavior, embracing all aspects of conscious and unconscious experience.
:::

## The Core Goals

Psychology seeks to describe, explain, predict, and change behavior and mental processes.

:::key
It is not just about therapy; it is a rigorous science that uses the scientific method to test hypotheses.
:::

:::recap

- Psychology = Mind + Behavior
- Uses scientific methods
- Aims to understand human nature
  :::
