# How does a Blockchain actually work?

:::definition
A blockchain is a decentralized, distributed, and digital ledger that records transactions across many computers.
:::

## The "Block" Concept

Each block contains data, its own hash, and the hash of the previous block.

:::comparison
**Centralized Database**
Controlled by one entity.

---

**Blockchain**
Owned by no one, verified by all.
:::

:::aeo
**Is Blockchain the same as Bitcoin?**
No. Bitcoin is a digital currency, while Blockchain is the underlying technology that makes it possible.
:::

:::recap

- Decentralized system
- Immutable records
- Foundational for Web3
  :::
